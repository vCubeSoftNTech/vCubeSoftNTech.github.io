Why is it taking so long for my project to upload?

Upload speeds are based on your wifi signal and your internet connection. Place your iPhone / iPod close to your router, or the source of wifi, to ensure a good signal. Keep in mind streaming media, or other activities such as video games may use up a considerable amount of your internet bandwidth and thus slow down the upload of your project.

How do I know if all my photos have uploaded successfully?

Once you select finish project, you will see a cloud icon with a progress bar appear next to your project, and a notification appears at the top of the screen letting you know your project is uploading. You must keep the InsideMaps Capture app window open until the upload is finished. If the app is minimized or closed the upload will pause. Keep in mind you must have access to wifi to upload, otherwise, the app will wait until a wifi connection is available. When the upload is complete the progress bar will disappear, and you will receive an email notification that your project has been submitted.

How much free space should I have on my iPhone / iPod?

At least 16gb is recommended.
